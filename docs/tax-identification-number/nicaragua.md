## Taxpayer identification number (RUC) 
In Nicaragua, for companies and individuals engaging in commercial activities, whether at a national or international level, it is mandatory to register in the Single Registry of Taxpayers and acquire a Taxpayer Identification Number, commonly referred to as RUC (registro Ãºnico de contribuyente) in Spanish. This registration is essential not only for local operations but also for facilitating import and export activities
